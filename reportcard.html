My Report Card

I would grade myself as Excellent in sharing and collaborating this semester. I consistently participated in my Writing Family, class discussions, and tutoring sessions with the intention of contributing meaningfully and supporting others. In my Writing Family, I not only participated regularly with my ideas. I shared my strategies and offered advice to help the group out during the semester. I must admit that participating in a discussion group is challenging when no one else is participating. Nonetheless I offered my input regularly. When others participated, I used their comments to strengthen my writing. I also made sure to respond respectfully and constructively to their input.

In class discussions, I stayed engaged by reading my classmates’ posts, responding to their ideas, and adding my own insights. Even when I was not fully confident in my writing, I showed up and participated because I understood that the class community depended on everyone contributing. These discussions helped me see writing from different perspectives and pushed me to think more deeply about audience and purpose.

Tutoring was another area where I demonstrated strong collaboration. I attended sessions with an open and honest mindset. I actively participated, owned my opportunities to improve, and continually acted on the advice I received throughout the remainder of the course. Throughout all three types of social activities I was engaged, agreeable and helpful. Such behavior satisfies expectations of the course as outlined in the course syllabus.

Based on the work I completed this semester; I earned an A in English 101. I completed all major assignments, participated actively in collaborative activities, and demonstrated consistent improvement in my writing. My essays show clear growth in organization, clarity, revision, and audience awareness. I revised my work thoughtfully, applied feedback from my teacher, peers, and tutors, and produced writing that reflects both effort and progress.

I also met the expectations for engagement by participating in Writing Families, contributing to discussions, and attending tutoring sessions. My reflections throughout the semester show that I can evaluate my own work honestly and articulate how I have grown as a writer.
